FAQ

Q. What is senior care? ▼
Senior care includes a range of services designed to support the health, safety, and daily living needs of elderly individuals, either at home or in specialized care facilities.
Q. Who needs senior care? ▼
Elderly individuals who face challenges with mobility, memory, chronic health conditions, or daily activities like bathing, dressing, and medication management may benefit from senior care.
Q. What types of senior care services are available? ▼
Services may include assisted living, nursing care, dementia/memory care, palliative care, companionship services, and medical monitoring.
Q. What kind of medical support do you provide? ▼
We provide regular health check-ups, medication administration, assistance with medical appointments, and emergency care when needed.
Q. What’s the difference between assisted living and nursing care? ▼
Assisted living supports daily activities in a community setting, while nursing care offers more intensive medical supervision and skilled nursing services.
Q. Can family members visit anytime? ▼
Yes, we encourage regular family interaction. Visiting hours are flexible, and we offer scheduled video calls for families living abroad.
Q. How are meals and nutrition handled? ▼
Our in-house dietitians create customized meal plans tailored to specific medical conditions such as diabetes, hypertension, and kidney health. All meals are freshly prepared and adhere to strict hygiene standards.
Q. What safety measures are in place at your facility? ▼
We have 24/7 security surveillance, fall-prevention systems, emergency response buttons, and trained staff available at all times.
Q. Is your care staff trained and certified? ▼
Yes, our caregivers and nurses are trained in geriatric care, first aid, dementia handling, and emergency response protocols.
Q. Do you provide support for NRI families with elders in Chennai? ▼
Yes, we offer complete elder care for families living abroad, including regular health updates, video calls, and emergency coordination.
